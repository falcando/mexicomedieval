Privacy notice

México Medieval, the website of Hervin Fernández Aceves, may collect contact data only when a person subscribes to the newsletter or communicates through the channels indicated on this site.

The data that may be collected are name and email address. In some cases, if the person chooses to provide them, institution, country, or contact message may also be collected.

Personal data will be used exclusively to send the México Medieval newsletter and, where applicable, to share information about courses, fairs, festivals, lectures, publications, and activities linked to the site.

The data provided will not be sold, rented, or used for purposes unrelated to the requested communication.

The newsletter may be sent through an external email management platform. In that case, the data will be processed only to manage the subscription and send the requested communications.

Subscribers may unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time through the link included in each email or by writing through the contact channels indicated on this site.

Interested persons may request access, rectification, cancellation, or opposition regarding their personal data through the contact channels indicated on the Contact me page.

This privacy notice may be updated to reflect changes to the site, the newsletter, or México Medieval activities. Any changes will be published on this page.
