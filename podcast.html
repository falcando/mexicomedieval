Mexico Medieval

A project of conversation and public history developed with Lancaster University and ARDEMAC.

The original season of México Medieval brings together episodes on historical reenactment, siege weapons, video games, medieval festivals, food, powerful women, the right of conquest, transitions of power, and medieval legends, designed to rediscover the Middle Ages from Mexico.
